About

{{page.intro}}

Hey there! My name is Caroline Portugal and I enjoy working at the intersection of design and engineering—acting as interaction designer, coder and visual designer all rolled together—because this is where I think fresh ideas and innovative solutions come from. I’m also interested in everything typography, SVGs and web animations.

A-side: Other past works include redesign and build for: Democracy International, Golden and Golden Dashboard. I was also part of the team designing and building Astro Digital while working for Development Seed.

Past life: I’m a trained Architect & Urban Planner and in my past life worked for Davis Brody Bond as an Architectural Designer on the project for the National Museum of African American History & Culture—by far the largest, most complex and meaningful project I’ve worked on in my life.
