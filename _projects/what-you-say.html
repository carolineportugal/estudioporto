--- layout: project title: What You Say, What You Show, What I See subtitle: Impressions on Communication season: Spring 2014 categories: Art Direction & Film description: "

A short movie that explores the different levels of communication that happen in one-on-one conversations. Firstly, on an objective level: the spoken word, what is being said deliberately. Secondly, the body communication: what is being shown and communicated by the speaker’s body parts movements and reactions, or lack of. And lastly, how the listener interprets the message: how the direct and indirect information being passed, combined with the receiver’s own experiences and subjective interpretations, are really what shapes the final product of communication.

" ---
